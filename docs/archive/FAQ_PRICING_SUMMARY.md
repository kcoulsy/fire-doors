## Category: Fire Door Inspections

### Q: How much does a domestic apartment entrance fire door inspection cost?

**A:** For domestic apartment entrance fire doors, our inspection service costs £50 plus VAT. This fee covers our time and expertise to visit your property and inspect your fire door in person. If you already have an FRA (Fire Risk Assessment) report that has flagged issues, we charge this fee because FRA reports are never detailed enough to form an accurate valuation - we need to see the door in person to assess the exact condition and provide precise pricing for the necessary works. Please note that most fire doors do fail inspection - this is completely normal. Fire doors move and are frequently used, which causes natural wear and tear over time. Our inspection identifies what needs attention so we can provide you with accurate pricing for repairs or maintenance.

### Q: What if I don't have an FRA report but need my door checked?

**A:** If you don't have an FRA report but need your apartment entrance fire door checked to satisfy management requirements or your legal obligations, we can provide a door inspection service. This will give you a comprehensive assessment of your fire door's condition, compliance status, and detailed recommendations. Contact us to discuss current pricing for this service and what's included in the inspection.
